## ***Introduction***

Carduino 328  is a microcontroller board based on the ATmega328. You can use it with any Longan board by plugging it into one of the edge connector slots. Carduino 328 supports all the Longan cards.

10-pin Connector
The 10-pin connector allows the Carduino to connect with sensors, actuators, and other devices.
